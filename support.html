Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my game progress?

To save your progress, you need to create a free Breaking Point Games account. Once you're logged in, all your progress is automatically saved to the cloud.

Are the games free to play?

Yes! All of our games are free to play. We offer optional cosmetic purchases in some games, but the core experience is always free.

What platforms are supported?

Most of our titles support cross-platform play across PC, Xbox, PlayStation, and mobile devices. Check the individual game page for specific platform availability.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

You can reset your password by visiting the login page and clicking the "Forgot Password?" link. An email will be sent to you with instructions.
